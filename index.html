Dream Vacation Destinations

Sydney

Sydney, Australia is home to over 100 beaches, as well as its famous, eponymous opera house.

Banff

Canada's Banff National Park has over 1,000 miles of hiking trails and its mountains range from 45-120 million years old.

Bora Bora

Part of French Polynesia, Bora Bora is home to only 9,000 residents in its three villages.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is over 13,000 miles long, which is half the length of the equator.

Prague

Prague has a rich history, showcased by its architecture, art, and cuisine. The Czech Republic is also the world leader in beer consumption per capita!

Iceland

Over 60% of Iceland's population lives in the capital, Reykjavik. The country also features hot springs that can be used year-round.

Japan

Japan is made up of 6,852 islands, and over 80% of its terrain is mountainous.

New Zealand

Home to the Maori people, New Zealand is a country known for its beautiful landscapes. The Lord of the Rings films were shot in New Zealand, and you can actually visit a Hobbit home.

New York City

New York City is a nexus of all cultures. Over 800 languages are spoken amongst its 8 million residents, and there are countless delicious cuisines to be enjoyed.

Seoul

With twice the population density of NYC, the capital of South Korea is one of the largest cities in the world. Seoul is a bustling cultural hub with the latest in technology and K-Pop juxtaposed with ancient architecture and cultural heritage.
